About

The inspiration for the Motor_Management_System project stemmed from the growing need for a streamlined, user-friendly solution to manage and sell vehicles in an increasingly digital marketplace. Observing the challenges faced by dealerships and individual sellers, such as maintaining inventory, tracking sales, and providing detailed vehicle information to potential buyers, highlighted the necessity for a comprehensive system. This project aims to simplify these processes, enhance customer experience, and ultimately drive sales growth by leveraging modern technology to address these industry pain points.


This is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
